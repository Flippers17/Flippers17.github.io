Disco Demolition Knight

6 weeks
This is a student project I did together with 11 other people in the course Spelprojekt 2 (Game project 2), where the focus was mostly just on making a fun game in order to improve your skills. The game is about surviving hoards of enemies, using different musical weapons, that fire in sync with the music.
