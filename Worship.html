Starstruck

This is a project that I made together with 4 other people and was made during a Game Jam lasting 48 hours with the theme Worship. In this game you play as a character that can shoot star-shaped projectiles and you try to free as many small mushroom guys as possible. These mushroom people gather around you and act as a shield, but can also be sacrificed at an altar in order to upgrade your character.
