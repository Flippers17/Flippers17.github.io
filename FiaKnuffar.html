Fia knuffar

This was made during my second ever game jam that I participated in, this time with 3 other people and, again, 48 hours to make it. The theme was “Fall” which in our interpretation was reflected in that the goal of the game was to make a bunch of people fall off a building… by pushing them. This time around, I had a lot more sleep and felt much more well rested than last time, although this led to me spending less time working on the game. In the end, that didn’t affect the results that much and, besides the horrible balancing, it didn’t feel like there was something crucial that we didn’t have time to finish before the deadline.

Some of my contributions:

  • Pushing mechanic and related quick time events
  • Player Movement
  • Enemy Behaviour
    • Outside of programming:
  • Lighting
  • Level Design
  • Gameplay design
